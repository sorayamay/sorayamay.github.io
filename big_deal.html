Big Deal

A steamy contemporary romance coming June 1st on Amazon

What's the price of love?

Tom Macaulay is a financial wizard, a master of the market. He plays to win, and the game always goes his way. But to stay ahead of his rivals, he must pull off the biggest deal of his career - and to do it, he needs new ideas and a new perspective.

Fresh out of college, Veronica 'Ronnie' Haas wants to prove she can settle down, be an adult and hold down a job. Tom wants her street-smarts and computer genius to make his deal happen - and when they're thrown together, he wants her for much more.

But every game has winners and losers. When Ronnie learns the real cost of the deal, she has to make a choice: will she follow her conscience, or follow her heart?
