About

Soraya May likes happy endings, expensive whisky, and typography. She has a well-tempered husband, and an ill-tempered cat.

'Professor Trouble', Soraya's debut romance, features a hot guy from England, a funny, feisty heroine, plenty of steam, and an HEA!
